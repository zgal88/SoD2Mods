# Mods

 **Boom Pack** - Inspired By/Based off of [Siimpsons X-Plosive Pyro Pack](https://www.nexusmods.com/stateofdecay2/mods/48)  
- Stack capacity increased to 999 for explosives  
- Decreased explosives weight to 0  
- Increased damage done to ferals, bloaters  
- Fire from explosives now affects armored zeds as well.  
- Added all the non-pyro explosives too, including anniversary cake and DLC explosives.        

 **Weightless Weapon Mods**  
- Decreases all weapon mods weights to 0, including tumbler and accelerator mods.   


 **Modder Appreciation Shirts**  
- Replaces eight of the Layered T-Shirts with alternate variants, sporting logos and icons from popular modders and members of the official SoD2 Modding Discord Server!
